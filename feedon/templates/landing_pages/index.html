Intro

FeeDon is a simple service that converts your Mastodon timelines into RSS feeds. In essense, it enables you to use your RSS reader as a Mastodon client, nicely formatting your home, local, federated, and list timelines into Atom feeds.

Privacy Notes

Before getting started, please note that logging in with this service will store the following information on the hosting server:

  • Your Mastodon handle
  • An authorization token for your account granting read-only access to your timelines
  • Metadata about the lists you've created
  • Metadata about your Mastodon instance

While this service will not store any posts, your account's access token will grant it access to read your timelines, which includes follower-only posts and DMs. Make sure you trust your admin before authenticating!

If you want to be super duper careful, you can always self-host by setting up your own instance of FeeDon. See the project's SourceHut repository for details.
